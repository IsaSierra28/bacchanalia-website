About Bacchanalia

Where Passion Meets Tradition

Trained at the Stratford Culinary School in Ontario, our chef embarked on a journey that went far beyond the art of cooking.It was a pursuit of culture, memory, and the deep human need to gather around a table.

After years of dedication in professional kitchens, he now brings that vision to life through Bacchanalia. A space named after the ancient Roman festival in honor of Bacchus, god of wine and revelry. Here, every dish tells a story, every glass carries a tradition, and every guest is welcomed as part of something greater than a meal.
